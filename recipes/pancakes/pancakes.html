Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a traditional famous meal of batter bread combined with cream, milk, fruit, and so on.

Required ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Lemon
  • Chocolate
  • Sugar

Cooking steps

  1. Mix flour, eggs and milk
  2. Fry a mixture on a pan for 10 minutes
  3. After the frying is done, pour the chocolate over the pancakes
