Pizza

Description

Pizza is a tasty Italian dish served as a round dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, meat and other ingredients.

Required ingredients

  • Dough
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato

Cooking steps

  1. Combine fluor and salt and add olive oil to the mixture
  2. Knead for about 10-15 minutes
  3. Let the dough rise for about 24 hours
  4. Carefully shape the dough
  5. Put the ingrediens on the dough
  6. Bake the mixture for about an hour
